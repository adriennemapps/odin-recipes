Almond Berry Smoothie Bowl

Description

Almond milk and almond butter are the star ingredients in this berry smoothie for a nutritious, on-the-go meal that is vegan and paleo-friendly.

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • water as needed

Directions

  1. Combine blueberries, banana, almond milk, and almond butter in the blender
  2. Blend until smooth, adding water for thinner smoothie.
